CIRCUIT DEBUG

An attack of bugs has been reported recently; the only weapon you have to protect yourself is your brain. Think, think, and think again as your every move will define your ultimate destiny in this game of circuit debugging.

Start building your team of warriors before the bugs start to consume every circuit around. All teams will get a faulty circuit which you will debug in the given time. There will be 3 rounds and in each round, a circuit will be given to each team. As soon as a team debugs the given circuit they will be promoted to the next round. The team that completes the final round first will be the winning team. The event will take place via online meeting platforms.

So, put on your thinking hats and start killing the bugs!

Rules:

Round 1 : One liners /MCQ

Round 2 : Debug The given circuit-Level Easy

Round 3 : Debug The given circuit - Level Hard

All the Questions of various rounds will be projected on the Screen.

Judging Criteria: Based on the score of participants.(time + accuracy)

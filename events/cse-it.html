Hogwarts: The Wizards Unite

The Triwizard Tournament was first established some seven hundred years ago as a friendly competition between the three largest European schools of wizardry: Hogwarts, Beauxbatons and Durmstrang. A champion was selected to represent each school, and the three champions competed in three magical tasks. The schools took it in turns to host the tournament once every five years, and it was generally agreed to be a most excellent way of establishing ties between young witches and wizards of different nationalities.

So this year you get a chance to participate in this prestigious tournament. But be ready to answer a few quirky questions!! Before the main tournament there is preliminary round followed by the 3 main stages:
All the 4 levels including the preliminary one will have a Harry Potter related question followed by a coding question. You'll have to choose a Harry Potter question answering which correctly (under 1 minute) will earn you a hint for your coding question.

Beware of cheating as Dumbledore's watching you!!

CODE HEIST

“The most important moments are the ones that make you realize there’s no turning back. You’ve crossed a line, and you’re stuck on the other side now.” - Tokyo.

Do you have the guts to cross the line?
You are helping The Professor rob "The Royal Mint of Spain".

Challenges will be thrown at you throughout the course of the game. On solving each challenge, you will reach a new level in your heist. Each round brings you closer to pulling off the greatest heist of the century. All the answers carry a secret way to escape from the bank and reach The Professor.

The first 3 teams to finish the Heist, win. So let the Code Heist begin!!!!!

Instructions:

1. Players have to login to the game using the password provided to them.
2. There will be a total of 4 rounds in the game.
3. Each round can have 1-4 questions/puzzles.
4. After solving each question, follow the hints/instructions that manipulate your answer to give a code + a numeric value.
5. Google the code to get the name of a money heist character.
6. The final answer in all rounds is a name + a numeric value.
7. Remember all your answers as they contain the secret to reach the end of the heist.
8. Each team gets 2 chances per answer (irrespective of the no. of devices you are logging in through).
9. After the 4 rounds follow the given instruction to end the heist.


FIND THE VACCINE

Team Size : Single Player.

Our world is facing the threat of this virus. The pandemic has affected most of the human population. Looking into this, the government decided to send a team of immensely talented doctors across the world to the most affected parts of the world. And you are one of them.

You will be assigned a country where almost 50% of the population is already infected. You are supposed to utilize your skills to solve the questions given, in order to unlock the vaccine injections to cure them.

Your target is to cut the percentage of infected people as much possible and save that country from this pandemic. But this virus is highly contagious and spreads from one person to another through various modes. Hence you will have to solve the given question in the given time otherwise the diseases will keep on spreading infecting more people. Your country might even turn into a red alert zone, but don’t worry, you will be given an option to call our special forces for your help. As your region turns red an ambulance will reach to you with a bonus question that will help you cut off your infected population and remove the red alert.

After you successfully solve all the stages of questions, your region will finally become uninfected and will turn green. Each player is expected to try their best to get people out of this pandemic.

Saviours, get ready to save the world!

THE NAVAL CODE

A vast and treacherous ocean.
One mystical treasure.
And you are not the only one seeking the riches of the treasure of Avery Henry.

Join this game loaded with strategy and coding, to battle with many more savage pirates seeking the treasure at the end. Attack enemy ships with cannonballs of coding questions and if you are struck yourself, solve the question to gain treasure coins. If you fail to answer the question correctly or if the timer runs out, you lose health points. Should you lose all your health points, your voyage in this game shall end.

A ship can’t be attacked more than once at the same time, so keep up the battle but don’t gang up on anyone ;-) You will start with 100 health points and 0 treasure coins. The treasure shall go to the one who collects the largest bounty(treasure coins) and wins the game!

Wishing you a fair wind ever and always!

Instructions :

1. Players have to login to the game using the credentials provided to them.
2. Every question would have a time limit of 15 minutes.
3. A ship that has already been attacked can’t be attacked again.

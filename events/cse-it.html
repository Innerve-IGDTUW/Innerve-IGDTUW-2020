MECHATHON

An engineer gives solutions to problems varying widely in their applications and significance.

If you wish to enhance your problem-solving skills then come and participate in Mechathon. The participants will be provided with critical real-life problems.

Working in teams of 2 to 6 students, they will come up with a machine based approach to solve the problem. The problem statements will be provided via mail to the registered participants on 21st October 2020. The teams can choose to work on any one of them and develop an ingenious solution for it. Prototype and CAD models are allowed. The teams can include any videos or audios related to their solution in the final presentation.

The parameters evaluated are: originality, relevance to the statement, feasibility and CAD models. So, gear up engineers-to-be and activate your skills.

1. There is no restriction in the number of teams participating from each Institute. 2. Each team should comprise a maximum of 6 members including the team leader. 3. Valid id proofs(College ID and Aadhar Card) must be submitted by each member of the group. 4. Webcam and microphones must be on at all times while presenting the solution. 5. The teams cannot make any changes in the application once they have submitted the registration amount. 6. The teams must adhere to the deadlines strictly. No applications will be entertained after the deadline. 7. The solution cannot contain content that is sexually explicit, violent, derogatory of any ethnic, racial, gender, religious, professional or age group, which violates any law, or which communicates a message inconsistent with the positive good will to which we wish to associate.

11. The participants should have good internet connectivity. However, if a team loses their internet connectivity in the middle of playing the game, they won't be allowed to compete with other teams further.

SHARK TANK

Show your entrepreneurial potential,
develop and present business models,
net the shark,
and grab the investment.

Race against time to give presentations in limited time and leave a lasting impression on the judges. You can participate in teams of 2 to 5 people. The competition will have two rounds: the preliminary round and the final round.

The preliminary round would last for 5 minutes for each team, testing the team's idea. The final round would take 10 minutes plus a Q&A round for each team and involve a deeper analysis of the business plan through a presentation.

Judging criteria: Online Business Plan, Ideation, Market Applicability, Future Scope, and Scalability.

Do you think you can create a business plan that links all these and is good enough to fish investors for your startup?

Find out whether you can bait the shark or would you get swallowed instead.

All the best!

Participation: Team participation (2-5)
Date: 7-8 November
Starting time: 12 pm
Platform: Google Meet

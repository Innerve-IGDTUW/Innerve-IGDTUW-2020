DEBATE COMPETITION

Social media is addictive precisely because it grants us something that we think we might lack in real-world: a value as an individual. 'The Social Dilemma' on Netflix has intrigued many minds across the globe.

We now present an opportunity for you to speak your mind in the debate competition - Attention: To Pay or Not to Pay.

Participants will be sent an email regarding the topics of the debate and the roles they will be assigned. Keep in mind, you will have to present your viewpoints based on the role assigned to you. The debate will test the convincing ability of the participants since the audience and other competitors can interject. Each participant will have 2 minutes to present and 5 minutes will be allotted for interjection.

Platform: Google Meet
Time: 11 am
Date: 7 November, 2020

All students from schools and colleges can participate and get the chance to win prizes worth Rs.8,600

Are you ready for this stimulating challenge for the mind!
